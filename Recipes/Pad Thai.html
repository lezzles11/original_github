Pad Thai

Difficulty: Easy Source: The Chefs Knowledge

Ingredients

250 g pack medium rice noodle

2 tsp tamarind paste

3 tbsp fish sauce

2 tsp sugar

1 garlic clove

3 spring onions

2 tbsp vegetable oil

1 egg

200 g large cooked prawn

75 g beansprout

Peanuts for garnish

Lime for dressing the salad

Directions

Boil water and pour on the noodles in a large bowl to soften for 5 minutes then drain well.

Mix the tamarind paste, fish sauce and sugar in a small bowl.

Chop the garlic finely. Cut the spring onions into 1cm long.

Heat a wok (or large frying pan) well before pour in the oil. Place garlic and spring onions and stir fry until they begin to soften for about 30 seconds. Put the cooked vegetables to the side of the wok and crack the egg in the centre to make a quick scrambled egg.

Add the prawns and beansprouts then the noodles. Once all mixed well, pour the fish sauce mixture and toss well.

Serve with chopped peanuts and wedges of lime.
