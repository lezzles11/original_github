Cold Marinated Peanuts

★★

Lesley

Source: Ken Hom

Ingredients

450g raw peanuts

3 tbsp light soy sauce

3 tbsp dark soy sauce

1 tbsp chopped garlic

1 whole star anise

150ml chinese white rice vinegar

85ml shaoxing rice wine

salt and black pepper

Directions

1. Blanch peanuts by immersing them in a saucepan of boiling water for 2 minutes
2. Drain and let cool, skins should come off
3. Put blanched peanuts into a bowl with all the other ingredients, with 2 tsp salt and black pepper
4. Let peanuts marinate for 2-3 hours, stirring from time to time
5. Serve as snack
