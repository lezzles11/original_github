Rice Topped with Chicken and Egg

★★★

Lesley

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Dashi and Umami

Ingredients

FOR THE STOCK:

140 ml dashi

100 ml mirin

4 tbsp soy sauce

400 g chicken thighs

12 eggs

1 spring onion

4 bowls of cooked rice*

Some sansho pepper

Directions

Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces. Holding your knife at an angle, finely chop the spring onion. Using 4 separate containers, break and whisk 3 eggs into each. Place the rice in a donburi (large rice bowl).

Heat the stock in a pan. Once boiled, add the chicken and cook on a medium heat for 3-4 minutes. Add the spring onions and heat for a little longer.

Place a quarter of (2) into a small pan and pour one persons portion of eggs around it. When half-cooked, transfer to the top of the rice in the donburi bowl and sprinkle with sansho pepper.

Repeat for the remaining three portions.
