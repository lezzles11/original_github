Baked Hong Kong Classic Pork Chop Over Rice

★★★

Lesley

Cook Time: 20 mins Source: https://www.homaydo.com/recipes/經典傳統港式焗豬扒飯/

Ingredients

450g boneless pork fillet


Marinade:

3 tbsp (15 ml) shaoxing rice wine

1 tbsp light soy sauce

2 tsp sesame oil

1 tsp cornstarch

8 green onions

1 tbsp peanut oil

1 tsp sugar

salt and black pepper


To fry pork chops:

50 g flour

1 egg


Tomato sauce:

1 tablespoon oil

½ onion

2 tomatoes

250ml water

250ml tomato sauce

1 tbsp sweetener


Fried rice:

Rice

2 tbsp (30ml) peanut or vegetable oil

3 tbsp (45ml) finely chopped fresh ginger

4 oz (100g) green onions, coarsely chopped

salt and black pepper

2 tbsp (30ml) finely chopped cilantro


Directions

Marinade pork chop for 30 minutes

Put 50 g of flour in a plate, and an egg in another bowl

Place pork chop in flour, then place it in egg

Heat wok, add oil

Fry each side for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown.

Set pork aside (put it on paper towel)

Next, make the fried rice by heating wok over high heat until it is hot.

Add the oil, and when it is hot, add the ginger, tum down the heat and slowly stir-fry until the ginger has browned.

Add 3 tbsp green onions, 2 tsp (10ml) of salt and ½ tsp (2ml) of black pepper and stir-fry for 2 minutes.

Then add the rice and continue to stir-fry for 5 minutes, or until it is heated through. Finally, add the 2 tbsp cilantro.

Give the mixture several good stirs, then place onto plate.

Now heat the wok again, and add a little oil. Add 1/2 onion and 2 cut up tomatoes - sauté until soft and set aside.

Next, pour 1 tablespoon of oil and 1 tablespoon of flour. Stir fry over low heat until it becomes a paste.

Add 250ml of tomato sauce and mix well.

Add 250ml of water and simmer until tender. Add salt and 1 tablespoon of sweetener.

Add previously cooked fried onions and tomatoes.

Now place the rice in the plate, followed by the fried pork chop, followed by the sauce. Sprinkle with cheese if you would like.

Bake for 5 minutes.

Notes

Used the green onion recipe for the marinade
