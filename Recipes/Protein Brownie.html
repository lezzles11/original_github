Ingredients

1.5 scoops (70g) of chocolate Whey Protein Powder

4 tbsp (28g) coconut flour

2 tbsp (10g) unsweetened cocoa powder

1-2 tsp baking powder

1 tbsp stevia or 0 cal sweetener

2 tbsp (31g) sugar-free chocolate flavored syrup

1/4 cup (4g) 0% fat greek yogurt

3 tbsp (46g) egg whites

1 tbsp (14g) chocolate chips

Description

Too dry

Directions

1. Pre-heat the oven to 360F/180C

2. Add and mix all ingredients in a bowl.

3. Melt the chocolate chips or chop them into smaller pieces before mixing it in with the rest of the recipe.

4. Place a baking sheet into a baking pan or baking loaf and pour in the batter.

5. Bake for 7-10 minutes or until fully baked.

6. Let it cool for at least 10 minutes, cut into 4 brownies and enjoy!

Nutrition

Per serving (1 brownie 1/4 of recipe): Calories: 118 Protein:13g
Carbs: 10g Fats: 3g
