Nobu's Japanese Egg Custard (98 Kcal)

★★★★★

Dad, Lesley

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

400 ml dashi

70 g chicken thigh, boneless and skinless

2 shiitake mushrooms

2 eggs

1 tbsp & ½ tsp light soy sauce

1 tsp soy sauce

Some sake

Some salt


4 ginkgo nuts (optional)

Directions

Sprinkle the chicken with a 1/2 Tbsp soy sauce

Cut off the stalks of the shiitake mushrooms.

Beat the egg well then mix with the cool dashi then strain. Season with the light soy sauce.

Place 2 pieces of chicken, and mushroom into each serving bowl and pour on the egg mixture.

Put plastic wrap over bowls.

Place the bowls inside a pre-heated steamer and put the lid on. Cook on a medium heat for 2-3 minutes. When the surface and sides are starting to become firm, reduce the heat to medium low.

After 7-8 minutes, pierce with a skewer, and if a clear juice rises up through the hole, it is done. If not, steam on a low heat for a further 3-5 minutes.

Notes

Use kombu and make pure broth
Try to make less liquid next time

Optional:

Remove both the thick outer and the thin inner skin of the ginkgo nuts and cut in half lengthways.

Place the lily bulb and ginkgo nuts in a steamer and steam for 4-5 minutes, then sprinkle with a light layer of salt.
