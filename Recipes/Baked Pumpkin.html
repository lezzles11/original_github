Baked Pumpkin

★★★

Lesley

Prep Time: 3 min Cook Time: 15 min Difficulty: Easy Source: Tokyo Cult Recipes

Ingredients

1 teaspoon sunflower oil

1 teaspoon soy sauce

1 teaspoon honey

1 pinch salt

4 x 5 mm (¼ in) slices peeled pumpkin

1 rounded tablespoon panko breadcrumbs

Directions

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Combine the oil, soy sauce, honey and salt in a bowl. Add the pumpkin and mix to coat. Lay the slices of pumpkin on a baking tray and sprinkle the panko on top. Bake for 8–10 minutes until tender.
