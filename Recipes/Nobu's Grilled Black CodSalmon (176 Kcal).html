Nobu's Grilled Black Cod/Salmon (176 Kcal)

★★★★★

Dad, Lesley

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

6 oz. (180 g) filet of black cod

Miso

Directions

Cover the cold fillet with some of the miso in a container, marinate for 40 minutes, and take the fillet out. Cover the cucumber and radish with some of the paste in a different container as well.

Roast the black cod fillet in a 300°F (150°C) oven for about 13-15 (dependent on size) minutes. Take out from the oven, remove any bones, and sear under a salamander or broiler.

Notes

Leave skin on, leave paste on
Don’t use older salmon. Need to pan fry longer.
