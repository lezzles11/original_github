Aubergines with Tofu

★★★★

Lesley

Difficulty: Easy Source: Keith Floyd

Ingredients

3 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped

1 eggplant / aubergine

115 g/4 oz firm tofu, cut into 1 cm/½ inch cubes

2 large, fresh red chillies, finely chopped

1 tablespoon yellow bean sauce

15 basil leaves

vegetable oil, for frying

Directions

Heat some oil in a heavy-based wok or frying pan and quickly fry the garlic until very light brown.

Add the aubergines and tofu and cook for another 5–7 minutes, turning to brown all sides.

Add all the remaining ingredients and mix gently to heat through. Add hot water to simmer until eggplant is soft.

Serve immediately.
