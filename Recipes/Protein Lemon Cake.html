Protein Lemon Cake

★★★★

Lesley

Source: Pernilla

Ingredients

3/4 cup (121g) egg whites

0.5 tsp vanilla extract

1/4 cup (50g) stevia or sweetener of own choice

3/4 cup (60g) oat flour

1 scoop of 1 Up Nutrition Blueberry Muffin or Vanilla Protein

Powder (or protein powder of own choice) 1 tsp baking powder


FOR THE FROSTING

2 tbsp (16g) sugar-free fat-free vanilla jello

1 cup (212g) 0% fat free greek yogurt

2 tbsp stevia or 0 cal sweetener of own choice 1 tbsp lemon zest

Directions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F/175 °C
2. In a bowl add egg whites, vanilla extract, 1/4 cup (50g) stevia and 1 tbsp lemon juice.
3. With an electric whip, beat the egg whites until stiff (may take between 3-10 minutes)
4. Place a sift on top of the bowl, add the rest of the ingredients and softly mix the batter.
5. Place the mixed batter into a baking pan and bake in oven for 10-14 minutes.
6. While the cake is baking mix all frosting ingredients and place in fridge.
7. When the cake has been baked, let it cool off for 10 minutes and frost the
lemon cake. Sprinkle extra lemon zest on top of the cake if you like and ENJOY!
