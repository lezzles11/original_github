Seasonal Steamed Vegetables

★★★★★

Lesley

Source: youtu.be

Ingredients

Baby Corn

Sweet Potatoes

Pumpkin

Cabbage - rinse and soak

Broccoli

Cauliflower

Mushrooms


Optional:

Sugar snap peas

Squash

Asparagus

Directions

1. Cut and wash
2. Soak in salt for 2-3 minutes
3. Wait to dry on towels
4. Put in bags

For the spring ingredients, start with the potatoes, and once the potatoes are almost tender (after about 6 to 8 minutes), add the carrots and cauliflower and cook for about 2 minutes. Finally, add the shiitakes, asparagus, and sugar snap peas and cook for about 2 minutes longer.

For the autumn ingredients, arrange all the ingredients on the grate and steam until everything is cooked through, about 6 to 8 minutes.

Serve immediately at the table with your choice of the dipping sauces.

Notes

Steam starchy first (potatoes, pumpkin)
then steam the other stuff
Don’t put lotus
