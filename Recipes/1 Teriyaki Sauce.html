1 Teriyaki Sauce

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

60 ml (2 fl oz/¼ cup) light soy sauce

60 ml (2 fl oz/¼ cup) mirin (rice wine)

60 ml (2 fl oz/¼ cup) sake

juice of 1 lemon

40 g (1½ oz) caster (superfine) sugar

Directions

Combine all of the ingredients in a small saucepan. Bring to the boil, then simmer over low heat for 5 minutes until thick and syrupy.
