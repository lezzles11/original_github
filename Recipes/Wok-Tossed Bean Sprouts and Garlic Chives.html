Wok-Tossed Bean Sprouts and Garlic Chives

★★★★

Lesley

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4-6 Source: The Songs of Sapa

Ingredients

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic

2 red Asian shallots, finely chopped

300 g (10½ oz) bean sprouts

50 g (1¾ oz/1 bunch) garlic chives, roots trimmed, cut into 5 cm (2 inch) lengths

2 spring onions (scallions), cut into 5 cm (2 inch) lengths

1 tablespoon fish sauce

1 teaspoon sugar

½ teaspoon sesame oil

Directions

Place a wok over high heat, add the vegetable oil, garlic and shallots and stir-fry for 1 minute, or until fragrant.

Add the bean sprouts, garlic chives and spring onions and toss for 1 minute.

Add the fish sauce, sugar, 1 tablespoon of water and a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper.

Toss for a further minute, then finish with a drizzle of sesame oil. Serve with jasmine rice.
