Tomato and Minced Meat

★★★★

Lesley

Ingredients

- 800g Tomato

- 150g minced pork

- 1 slice ginger

- 2 cloves garlic

- 1 cup water


2 tsp sugar

1/2 tsp salt


1/2 TB oil

1/2 tsp sugar

1 tsp corn starch

2 tsp light soy sauce

Directions

1. Boil tomatoes for a minute and then peel
2. Cut tomatoes into pieces, removing hard portion of core
3. Pound slice of ginger and 2 cloves garlic. Add 2 tsp of sugar, 1/2 tsp of salt.
4. Marinate minced pork with 1/2 Tb oil, 1/2 tsp of sugar, 1 tsp of corn starch, 2tsp of light soysace. Mix the minced pork with seasoning well.
5. Heat the ginger, garlic and 1 TB of oil over high heat. Stir fry tomato. Add 1 cup of water.
6. Add minced pork and mix it well. Stir fry for five minutes.
7. After 5 minutes, crush tomato. Cover it again.
8. After 2 minutes, dish it up and serve
