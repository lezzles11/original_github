Sit-fried Pepper Beef With Snow Peas

Source: Ken Hom

Ingredients

450g beef fillet

2 tsp light soy sauce

2 tsp shaoxing rice wine

2 tsp sesame oil

2 tsp cornstarch

100g red or green bell peppers

3 tbsp peanut oil

225 snow peas

150ml chicken stock

3 tbsp oyster sauce

Directions

1. Cut beef into 2 in long
2. Put into a bowl, and add soy sauce, rice wine, sesame oil, cornstarch, 0.5 tsp salt and 0.25 tsp black pepper
3. Mix and marinate for 15 minutes
4. Cut red / green bell peppers into 2 in strips
5. heat wok, add oil, and when it is slightly smoking, stir fry beef for 3 minutes
6. Remove beef and drain colander
7. Wipe wok clean, reheat and return 1 tbsp drained oil
8. Stir fry bell pepper strips and snow peas for 2 minutes
9. Add chicken stock and oyster sauce and bring mixture to a boil
10. Return the cooked beef to wok and stir well
