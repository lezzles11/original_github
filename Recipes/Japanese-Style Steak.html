Japanese-Style Steak

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 25 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Tokyo Cult Recipes

Ingredients

4 x 150 g (5½ oz) steaks, ideally well-marbled and about 2 cm (¾ in) thick

salt

2 tablespoons sake

5 cm (2 in) white radish (daikon), grated

SAUCE

5 tablespoons soy sauce

2 tablespoons rice vinegar

juice of ½ organic lemon

1 garlic clove, crushed

Directions

Mix the sauce ingredients together. Take the steaks out of the refrigerator at least half an hour before cooking. Lightly season with salt, then cook the steaks in a frying pan according to your preference. Sprinkle over the sake to add aroma to the steak. Take the steaks off the heat and let them rest for 3 minutes. Cut into cubes and serve on a plate with the grated white radish and the sauce. Dip the steak into the sauce and eat with a little white radish.
