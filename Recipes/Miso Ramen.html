Miso Ramen

Dad To Try

Source: Signe Johansen

Ingredients

150g packet miso ramen noodles

1 egg

1 tbsp brown miso paste

1 spring onion

1 green chilli

Sesame seeds

Toasted sesame oil

Seaweed

Directions

1. Cook ramen noodles, add frozen peas
2. Cook egg by poaching it in broth
3. Add miso paste to noodles and stir
4. Remove from heat and garnish with chilli, spring onion, sesame seeds, sesame oil, etc.
5. Add seaweed
