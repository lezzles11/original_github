Macaroni Salad

★★★

Lesley

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 30 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 Source: Tokyo Cult Recipes

Ingredients

100 g (3½ oz) dried macaroni

1 x 80 g (2¾ oz) can of tuna in vegetable oil

4 tablespoons mayonnaise

salt and freshly ground pepper

½ cucumber, very thinly sliced

¼ onion, very finely chopped

½ slice of ham, cut into matchsticks

Directions

Cook the macaroni according to the instructions on the packet, then drain and leave to cool. Drain the tuna and flake with a fork. Combine the tuna with the mayonnaise and season with salt and pepper. Mix the cucumber with a pinch of salt and set aside for 5 minutes, then squeeze out the excess liquid with your hands. Soak the onion in a bowl of water for 5 minutes to soften its flavour, then drain well. Mix all the ingredients together thoroughly before serving.

Notes
