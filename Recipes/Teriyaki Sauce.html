Teriyaki Sauce

Source: Pro Home Cooks

Ingredients

2 cloves of garlic

a knob of ginger

sake

mirin

soy sauce

sesame seeds

corn starch

Directions

Mince garlic and ginger

Medium low heat, put in oil

Put in garlic and ginger

Cook for 3 minutes

Equal parts sake, mirin, soy sauce

Turn heat up, until it simmers

Taste it, and see if you need to add sweetener / sugar

Make slurry: 2 Tbsp corn starch and 4 Tbsp water

Slowly drizzle slurry

Add sesame seeds

Put everything in jar, can store for few months
