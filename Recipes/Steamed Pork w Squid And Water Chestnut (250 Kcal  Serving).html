Steamed Pork w Squid And Water Chestnut (250 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★★

Lesley

Source: letscookchinesefood.com

Ingredients

600 g Minced Pork

2 pieces Water chestnut

1 small squid

*can try putting salted egg

2 salted duck eggs

Seasoning:

1 Tbsp Oil

1 Tbsp cornstarch

1 tsp Sugar

1 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce

1/2 Tbsp Fish sauce

Directions

Soak, then peel and rinse Water Chestnut (2pcs).

Cut water chestnut finely (Can put in food processor)

Wash squid cleanly and cut finely.

Mix the chopped squid and water Chestnut

Add Seasoning: Light Soy Sauce (1 tbsp), Fish Sauce (1/2 tbsp), Sugar (1 tsp), Corn Starch (1 tbsp)

Add 1 tbsp of oil.

Mix the seasoning well. Then, mix it with minced pork (600 g of pork)

Stir in one direction until sticky.

Bring water to boil. Then, steam the Pork Patty with high heat for 15-18 mins.

Serve!

Notes

Don’t overcook! Don’t use pork from marketplace. Make sure you aren’t missing any ingredients here.
