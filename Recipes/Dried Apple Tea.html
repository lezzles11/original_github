Dried Apple Tea

★★★★★

Dad, Lesley

Ingredients

dried apple slices

water

Directions

Put 2-3 dried apple slices into 300 ml to 50 ml boiled water (75-85℃) or eat it right away.

2. Wait for 5 to 10 minutes, and can brew for 2 to 3 times.

3. Add honey or sugar if preferred.

4. Go well with French rose, hawthorn, etc.
