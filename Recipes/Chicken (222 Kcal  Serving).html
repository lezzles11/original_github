Chicken (222 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★★

Lesley

Ingredients

A whole chicken / 1 只整鸡

4 tablespoons salt / 4 汤匙盐，分开

Cornstarch / 淀粉，适量

Shaoxing wine / 2 汤匙绍兴酒，分开

Sesame seeds 烤芝麻，用于装饰

Directions

1. Wash chicken, very cleanly (remove everything from inside). Pat it dry.
彻底清洗鸡肉，去除内脏。再次洗净并用纸巾擦干。
2. Apply salt and cornstarch everywhere
在鸡肉上均匀涂抹盐和淀粉。
3. Cut off legs (below the knee) and the head. Rinse and pat dry again.
切掉鸡腿（在膝盖以下的部分）和头部。再次冲洗并用纸巾擦干。
4. Place 2 tablespoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of shaoxing wine into chicken. Rub it everywhere.
将2汤匙的盐和1汤匙的绍兴酒混合，然后将其均匀地涂在鸡肉上。
5. Pat it dry. Place another 2 tablespoons of salt and 1 tablespoon of shaoxing wine into chicken. Rub it everywhere again.
用纸巾擦干 - 将2汤匙的盐和1汤匙的绍兴酒混合，然后将其均匀地涂在鸡肉上(第二次）
5. Let it marinate for a couple of hours
让鸡肉静置，使其充分吸收香味。
6. Place in steamer (over a hot stove) for 15 minutes on high heat.
腌制后，将鸡肉放入蒸锅中，用高火蒸15分钟。
7. After 15 minutes, DO NOT OPEN COVER. Leave it in for another 15 minutes.
15分钟后，让鸡肉继续在蒸锅中（不要打开盖子）再蒸15分钟。
8. Open lid, and place chopstick through leg. Make sure no blood leaks (means it’s still raw)
为了检查鸡肉是否熟透，将筷子插入鸡腿中。如果没有血水流出，那么鸡肉就煮熟了。
9. Remove all skin
鸡肉煮熟后，去掉所有的皮。
10. Peel chicken, and put roasted sesame seeds on it
最后，剥离鸡肉，并用烤芝麻装饰。
