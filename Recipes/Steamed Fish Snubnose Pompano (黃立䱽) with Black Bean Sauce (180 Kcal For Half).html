Steamed Fish Snubnose Pompano (黃立䱽) with Black Bean Sauce (180 Kcal For Half)

Source: letscookchinesefood.com

Ingredients

1 Flesh Fish (Snubnose Pompano)

1 sprigs Spring Onion


Seasoning:

1.5 Tbsp Salted black beans (rinsed and minced)

1/2 Tsp Sugar

1 Tbsp Oil

1/4 tsp Chicken Power

1/4 tsp Salt

1 tsp Ginger Juice  1 tsp

1.5 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce

1/2 tsp Cornstarch

Directions

Ask for cleaning when buying the fish. Rinse and wipe it to dry.

Mix seasoning together

Cut few slots on both sides of fish, then coat both side with the seasoning

Bring water to boiling, then put the fish to steam. Steam with high heat for 10 minutes, then let rest 2 mins. Discard the spring onion on top of Fish. Serve!

Notes

Make sure to steam on silver pan, add more ginger, dont put it in the fridge if you bought it the day of
- high heat when steaming
- Need to wipe VERY clean, very dry!
- wash right before you cook (30 mins before)

After buy from market, make sure to grate scales, then put tissue paper inside to dry it
Take off skin and bones when serving
