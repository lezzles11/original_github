Protein Ice Cream (144 Kcal / Half)

★★★★★

Lesley

Source: Pernilla

Ingredients

1 scoop (33.5g) Chocolate Peanut butter whey protein powder

2 tbsp (16g) sugar-free fat-free vanilla jello

2 tbsp (16g) PB2 (powdered peanut butter)

1/2 cup unsweened almond milk (or any other milk you have available)

1 tsp (3g) Xanthan gum (or Guar Gum) *important ingredient

2 tbsp (10g) unsweetened cocoa (optional, depending on the flavor)

1/2 cup (100g) frozen strawberries

1-4 tbsp stevia or 0 cal sweetener of own choice

3 cup (500g) of ice cubes

Description

Wow.

Directions

1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
2. Add bits of water and stir or shake to get the blending going. This may take a couple of minutes.
3. Once it's blending smoothly, let it blend for 5-ish minutes until it becomes a nice thick consistency. 4. Enjoy together with the brownies, blueberry muffins, berries, banana, sugar-free syrup and/or PB2
