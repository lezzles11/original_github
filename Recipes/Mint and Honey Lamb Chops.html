Mint and Honey Lamb Chops

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

8 large or 12 smaller lamb chops

1 tablespoon olive oil

2 cloves garlic, sliced very finely

2 tablespoons honey

2 tablespoons lemon juice (bottled is fine)

12 sprigs fresh mint

salt and pepper

EQUIPMENT

20 x 30cm roasting tin

saucepan

Directions

Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the lamb on both sides until cooked to your liking. Do this in batches. Remove, season the cooked lamb with salt and wrap in a foil parcel. This will help it to tenderise.

Add the garlic to the pan, allow it to brown then add the honey and lemon juice. Leave to bubble away for 2â€“3 minutes while you chop the mint and add it immediately to the pan. Stir in and season to taste.

TO SERVE:

Serve the warm chops on plates with the sticky juice poured over.
