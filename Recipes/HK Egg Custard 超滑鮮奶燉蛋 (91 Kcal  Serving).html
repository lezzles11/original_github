HK Egg Custard 超滑鮮奶燉蛋 (91 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★

Lesley

Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: Servings 4 - 5 Source: pressurecookrecipes.com

Ingredients

3 large eggs (~180ml in volume)

1 ½ cup (375ml) whole milk , divided

A pinch of salt

4 tablespoons (52g) – 5 tablespoons (65g) granulated sugar / sweetener

Directions

Melt sweetener in 250ml milk over stovetop

Remove pot from heat and pour in 125ml milk so it cools down

Beat 3 eggs in seperate bowl

Combine Ingredients: While whisking, slowly pour the sweet milk mixture in the large glass measuring cup. Mix well.

Pour in white cups, wrap with plastic wrap

Steam for 10 minutes

Put in fridge

Notes

Really need to mix the egg with whisk...
