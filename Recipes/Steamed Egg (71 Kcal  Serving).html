Steamed Egg (71 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★★

Dad, Lesley

Source: letscookchinesefood.com

Ingredients

3 pcs egg

1 spring onion

1/2 tsp sesame oil

1/2 tsp salt

1 cup room temp, boiled water

Soy sauce

Directions

1. Mix egg, sesame oil and salt
2. Add 1 cup of water
3. Transfer to dish, steam with plastic wrap on for 8 minutes
4. Check by poking toothpick in (if toothpick can stand vertically, it's set)
5. Add spring onion, soy sauce
