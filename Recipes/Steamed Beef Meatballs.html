Steamed Beef Meatballs

Source: Ken Hom

Ingredients

350g minced beef

2 egg whites

5 tbsp cold water

1.5 tbsp light soy sauce

1 tbsp sesame oil

3 tbsp fresh cilantro

3 tbsp spring onions

1 tsp cornstarch

2 tsp sugar

Directions

1. Mix beef in food processor
2. Add egg whites and cold water, mix for few more seconds
3. Add rest of ingredients and season with 1 tsp salt and 2 tsp black pepper
4. Mix for about 1 minute, until the mixture has become a light paste
5. Form mixture in 1.5 in balls
6. Set up a steamer, put meatballs on a heatproof plate
7. Steam for 15 minutes on low heat
