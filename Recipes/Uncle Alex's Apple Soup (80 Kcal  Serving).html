Uncle Alex's Apple Soup (80 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★★

Dad, Lesley

Source: Alex Chow

Ingredients

美國蛇果四個 4 Apples

西施骨5O元 Pork

豬約4O元 Pork

山班魚2-3條(视乎大小） Fish

紅蘿蔔一條（中Size) 1 Red Carrot

蜜棗三粒， 3 Jujube

薑二，三片 3 slices Ginger

Directions

一、首先用凍水，將豬展和豬骨汆水
二、將美國蛇果去芯，切成一嚿嚿
三、紅蘿蔔去皮，並切成一塊塊，厚度約三厘米
四、煮滾半煲水，加入蜜棗三粒，薑三片，及以上材料，再次煮滾，然後教中火煲半小時
五、用鹽清洗山斑魚
六、用油煎山班魚，每邊約3分鐘
七、加熱水煎魚的鍋裏，猛火煮至湯轉白色
八、把魚放進魚袋內，和已轉白色的魚湯及魚袋一起放進另一邊在煲的肉湯中
九 、待湯水重新再翻滾，然後轉中火煲1.5小時
備注：蘋果肉也可以用魚袋包裹一起放進湯內煮

0. Blanch the pork belly and pork bones
1. First, use frozen water to boil the pork belly and pork bones.
2. Remove the core from the American snake fruit and cut it into pieces.
3. Peel the carrots and cut them into pieces, about three centimeters thick.
4. Bring the water to a boil, add three candied dates, three slices of ginger, and the above ingredients, bring to a boil again, and then simmer over medium heat for half an hour.
5. Clean the rockfish with salt
6. Fry the mountain fish in oil (high heat) - heat up a pan, then when it’s hot, put in two slices of ginger - then fry 3 minutes per side
7. Put 1-2 cups of water in the pan. Heat the pan for frying fish and cook over high heat until the soup turns white.
8. Put the fish into the fish bag (tie the back properly!), and
9. put it into the broth on the other side together with the fish soup that has turned white
10. Wait for the soup to boil again, then turn to medium heat and simmer for 1.5 hours.
Note: The apple meat can also be wrapped in a fish bag and put into the soup to cook.
