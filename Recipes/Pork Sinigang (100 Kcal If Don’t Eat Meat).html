Pork Sinigang (100 Kcal If Don’t Eat Meat)

★★★★★

Lesley

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 1 hr Total Time: 1 hr, 15 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: spoonandswallow.com

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds pork spare ribs or country ribs

2 quarts water

2 packets of tamarind soup mix, such as Knorr, or 2 cups lemon juice

2 tablespoons fish sauce

1 whole tomato

1/2 medium onion

4 cups of cabbage, quartered

3 cups of fresh spinach

Directions

Slice spare ribs, or cut country ribs into 1-Inch chunks. Add water to pot. Then add pork, one packet tamarind mix or lemon juice, then the additional packet to taste, fish sauce, tomato and onion. Bring to boil over medium high heat, then reduce to simmer for 45 minutes, or until pork is tender and flaky.

And in cabbage and cook for 10-15 minutes more. Turn off heat, stir in spinach and let sit 3-5 minutes. Serve alone or over steamed rice.
