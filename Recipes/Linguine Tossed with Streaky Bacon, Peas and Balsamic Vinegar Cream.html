Linguine Tossed with Streaky Bacon, Peas and Balsamic Vinegar Cream

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

500 g linguine (see BASICS)

100 g frozen peas

800 g streaky bacon

8 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

400 ml cream

2 bunches sage, washed and chopped

salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions

To make the sauce, use a very large frying pan, big enough to later hold all the pasta (if you don’t have a large enough pan, use a big salad bowl to toss). Heat the pan and throw in the bacon, without adding any fat. Do not pour off any of the bacon fat while cooking. When the bacon browns slightly, deglaze with the balsamic vinegar, cream and frozen peas. Bring to the boil, then remove from heat.

Cook the linguine for three minutes in lots of salty boiling water.

Add the sage to the sauce and warm it.

Toss the pasta in the sauce and serve.

If you’re making this dish for two people, it works well to divide the ingredients and make individual portions in a small pan, serving immediately.
