Mashed Potatoes

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 Source: Jonathan Waxman

Ingredients

2 pounds (910 g) Yukon Gold potatoes

6 cloves garlic, peeled and left whole

Sea salt

2 tablespoons (60 ml) olive oil

½ cup (240 ml) heavy cream

¼ cup (½ stick/115 g) butter

Directions

Wash and trim the potatoes. Cut them into 2-inch (5 cm) blocks.

Put the potatoes and garlic in a pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil, then add 1 teaspoon salt. Reduce the heat to a murmur. Simmer slowly until the potatoes are tender, about 30 minutes. Pour off all but ½ cup (120 ml) of the cooking liquid. Return the potatoes to the heat and add the oil and cream.

Using an immersion blender or an old-fashioned potato masher if you don’t have an immersion blender, gently puree the potatoes, cream, oil, and garlic over the heat.

Add the butter and continue to puree, then taste for seasoning. Serve hot.
