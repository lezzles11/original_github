Chocolate Chip Banana Muffins

★★★

Lesley

Source: Pernilla

Ingredients

1 scoop protein powder (33g)

1 medium banana

1/2 cup (40g) oat flour (1 blend regular oatmeal)

4 tbsp 0% fat greek yogurt

3 tbsp (46g) egg whites

0.5 tsp vanilla extract

1 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp baking soda/powder

1 small tbsp (6g) sugar-free fat-free jello

Lily's dark chocolate baking chips

Directions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F/175 °C
2. Add all ingredients for the cupcake into a bowl and stir well. If you want your chocolate chips to be on the top wait with adding these till the batter is placed into the muffin liners.
3. Place 10 muffin liners into a muffin pan and divide batter into the liners (or however many you would like)
4. Bake for about 12-15 minutes or until your cupcakes are fully baked.
5. Let them cool off and enjoy!

Nutrition

Serving (1/10): Per se Protein: 4.3g Calories: 50 Carbs: 7.3g Fats: 0.7g
