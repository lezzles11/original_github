Stuffed Omelette

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 35 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

100 g (3½ oz) boneless chicken thigh

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 small knob of butter

½ onion, roughly chopped

4 mushrooms, thinly sliced

4 bowls of cooked rice

4 tablespoons tomato sauce (ketchup) + extra to serve

salt and pepper

OMELETTE

8 eggs

butter

Directions

Cut the chicken into 2 cm (¾ in) cubes. Heat the vegetable oil and butter in a frying pan on a medium heat, then add the chicken and onion. Sauté until they are cooked, then add the mushrooms and rice. If the rice grains have stuck together, sauté until they are separated and heated through. Add the ketchup, stirring to coat all the grains of rice. Season with salt and pepper if needed, then set aside in a bowl.

Make 4 individual omelettes. Beat together 2 eggs per omelette with a pinch of salt. Heat a frying pan on a medium heat and add a small knob of butter. Pour in the beaten eggs and spread them quickly over the base of the pan to make a thin, flat omelette. Lower the heat, place a quarter of the ketchup rice in the middle of the omelette and wrap the omelette around the rice. Repeat for each serving.

Place the omelettes on plates and serve with ketchup on top.
