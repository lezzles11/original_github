Brown Chicken Stock

Source: Heston Blumenthal

Ingredients

2.5 kg chicken wings

150 g onion

75 g carrot

100 g button mushrooms

2 cloves of garlic

Directions

Preheat oven to 200 C

Rub chicken wings with oil, then put in roasting try and place in oven for 1 hour, turning every 20 minutes to ensure they are golden brown.

Pour away fat and set wings aside.

Add water to tray and, using a wooden spoon, scrape the bits from the bottom of the pan. Reserve this liquid.

Heat a thin layer of oil in the bottom of a pressure cooker over high heat.

When oil is hot, add onions and cook until golden brown, stirring frequently to prevent onions from catching on the bottom of the pan (45 mins)

Add carrots and cook until they are soft and caramelised before adding mushrooms and garlic (20 mins)

Cook until mushrooms are soft and cooked through (10 mins)

Add roasted chicken wings and deglazing liquid.

Cover with 2kg cold tap water and bring to a simmer, skimming off impurities that rise to top.

Take pressure cooking off heat and leave to cool before removing lid.

Strain liquid through a sieve lined with two layers of wet muslin. Refrigerate overnight.

Scrape the layer of fat from the surface before using or freezing.
