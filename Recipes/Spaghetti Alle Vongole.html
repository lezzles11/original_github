Spaghetti Alle Vongole

Difficulty: Easy Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

400 g spaghetti

1 kg vongole (clams) - see note

2 cloves garlic-finely sliced, green germ removed

Extra virgin olive oil

Parsley

Salt

1 chilli-finely sliced

Directions

Heat the olive oil in a pan. Add the garlic and soften. Add the chilli and then turn up the heat and add the clams and put on a lid and shake.

When the clams have all opened - after 2-3 minutes take the pan off the heat.

Meanwhile cook the spaghetti. Drain the pasta and keep a little of the cooking water if the sauce is too thick (see cooking water).

Add the pasta to the pan and combine. Sprinkle the parsley over the top and serve.
