Steamed Spareribs With Black Beans

★★★★

Lesley

Source: Ken Hom

Ingredients

750g spareribs

120ml chicken stock

1.5 tbsp light soy sauce

2tsp dark soy sauce

2 tsp sesame oil

1 tbsp chopped ginger

3 tbsp black beans

2 tbsp garlic

2 tbsp green / red chilies

2 tsp sugar

1.5 tbsp shaoxing wine

salt and black pepper

Directions

1. Cut spareribs in 2 inch segments
2. Rub with salt and let sit in a bowl for 20-25 minutes
3. Fill large saucepan with water and bring to boil
4. Turn heat to low, add spareribs and simmer for 10 minutes
5. Drain and discard water
6. Mix ingredients together in large bowl with 1 tsp salt and pepper
7. Add spareribs, coating them well
8. Transfer to deep plate or dish
9. Steam on low heat for 1 hr, or until spareribs are tender
