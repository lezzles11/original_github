Directions

- Add 2 cups dashi and 1 tsp sugar in small saucepan
- Add 1 TB mirin and 1 TB soysauce and boil
__ Taste the soup and adjust taste with salt
__ Cut fish cake and green onions
__ Cut meat into bite sized pieces
__ Saute oil and green onion
__ Add meat. Add 1 TB sugar and 1 TB soy sauce. Turn off heat when caramalized.
__ Boil udon in bowl of hot water.
__ Put soup into bowl. put udon in bowl. Put meat and fish cake on bowl.
