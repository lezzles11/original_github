Stir-Fried Crab or Lobster in the Shell

Difficulty: Easy Source: Keith Floyd

Ingredients

1 large crab or lobster, about 2 kg/4½ lb, or 2 smaller ones of at least 1 kg/ 2¼ lb each

6 hot red chillies, deseeded and finely sliced lengthways

2 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped

2 thin slices peeled fresh root ginger, finely chopped

1½ teaspoons cornflour

125 ml/4 fl oz water

5 or 6 spring onions, trimmed and chopped

1 teaspoon mild chilli powder

1 heaped teaspoon soft brown sugar

peanut oil, for stir-frying

Directions

Heat a large wok, add a little oil and stir-fry the crab or lobster for 6-8 minutes.

Strain off any excess oil, add the sliced chillies, the garlic and ginger and stir-fry for about 30 seconds.

Mix the cornflour with the water, then add to the wok with the spring onions, chilli powder and sugar and cook, stirring all the time, until the sauce thickens, then serve.
