Pumpkin Salad (64 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★

Lesley

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: 15 min Difficulty: Easy Source: Tokyo Cult Recipes

Ingredients

200 g (7 oz) pumpkin

1 tablespoon black raisins

4 mint leaves, chopped

1 rounded teaspoon chopped onion

1 teaspoon olive oil

squeeze of organic lemon juice

1 pinch salt

1 small pinch curry powder

1 small pinch sugar

1 tablespoon mayonnaise

Directions

Peel and cut the pumpkin into 2 cm (¾ in) chunks. Place in a saucepan with 100 ml (3½ fl oz) of water and place over low–medium heat. Cover and cook the pumpkin in the steam from the water. If the water evaporates before the pumpkin is cooked, add a little more. While the pumpkin is still hot, combine with the rest of the ingredients except the mayonnaise. Once the pumpkin salad has cooled, stir in the mayonnaise.

Notes

Raisin is key
Can use curry paste
