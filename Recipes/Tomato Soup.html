Tomato Soup

★★★★★

Dad

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 3 hr Total Time: 3 hr, 20 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 Source: letscookchinesefood.com

Ingredients

3 Potatoes, 4 Carrots, Pork, 6 Tomatoes, 1 slice ginger, 1.5 Tbsp oil, Salt

Directions

Peel and cut carrots and tomatoes. Put everything together and boil.
