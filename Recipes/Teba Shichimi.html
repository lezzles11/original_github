Teba Shichimi

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 1 hr 20 Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Tokyo Cult Recipes

Ingredients

500 g (1 lb 2 oz) chicken wings

1 tablespoon coarse natural salt

1 garlic clove, grated

2 tablespoons sake

½ organic lime

shichimi*

Directions

Combine the chicken wings with the salt, garlic and sake in a bowl, working it into the meat with your hands. Cover and leave to marinate in a cool place for at least 1 hour. If it will be more than an hour before you cook the chicken, put it in the refrigerator. Cut the wings in 2 at the joint. Heat a frying pan on a medium heat and when it is hot, cook the wings on both sides until golden brown, about 7 minutes.

Arrange the chicken wings on a plate. Sprinkle with lime juice, shichimi and, if you like, salt. Serve immediately – these are even better with beer!
