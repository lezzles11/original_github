Calamari

Difficulty: Easy Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

250 g (9 oz) baby calamari

40 ml (1Â¼ fl oz) olive oil

2 teaspoons lemon juice

sea salt flakes

Directions

Clean the calamari leaving the skin intact. Separate the tubes from the tentacles. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over high heat, add the calamari tubes and cook for 1 minute, then add the tentacles and cook for another 20 seconds. Season with the lemon juice and salt. Serve immediately.
