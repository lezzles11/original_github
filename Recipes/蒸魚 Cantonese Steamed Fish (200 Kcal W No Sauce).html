蒸魚 Cantonese Steamed Fish (200 Kcal W/ No Sauce)

★★★★★

Lesley

Ingredients

Fish 馬友魚,䱽魚

Ginger

Garlic

Spring Onion

Soy sauce

Oil

Directions

1. Wash fish well, scale
2. Place paper towels inside the fish to dry
Put some salt on fish
3. Put ginger and green onion around and in fish
4. Steam on high heat for 8 minutes (depending on size)
5. Heat up the sauce pan
6. Place oil, ginger, and spring onion and fry
7. Take water out of fish

Notes

Leopard Coral Grouper (Red Grouper) 東星班
Red Seabream
Star Snapper 石
Red Snapper 紅魚
馬友魚
Mangrove Red Snapper
