Crab in Egg Custard

★★

Lesley

Source: Ken Hom

Ingredients

225g freshly cooked white crabmeat


FOR THE CUSTARD

4 eggs

1 ¼ cups (300ml) Chicken Stock

2 tsp (10ml) finely chopped fresh ginger

3½ tbsp (50ml) finely chopped green onions, white part only

1½ tbsp (22ml) Shaoxing rice wine or dry sherry

salt and white pepper


FOR THE GARNISH

2 tsp (10ml) dark soy sauce

2 tbsp (30ml) green onions, chopped

1 tbsp oil

Directions

1. Mix the custard ingredients in a bowl, season with 1 tsp of salt and white pepper
2. Add the cooked crabmeat. Mix well to blend all the ingredients together.
3. Put the mixture into a deep, heatproof dish. Next, set up a steamer, or put a rack into a wok or deep saucepan, and fill it with 2 in of water.
4. Bring the water to a boil over high heat. Carefully lower the dish with the crab mixture into the steamer or onto the rack.
5. Turn the heat to low and cover the wok or saucepan tightly.
6. Steam gently for about 18-20 minutes, or until the custard has set.
7. Remove the custard from the steamer, pour the soy sauce over the top and sprinkle with the green onions.
8. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a small saucepan until it begins to smoke, then pour this over the custard and serve at once.
