Stir-fried Spinach with Garlic

★★★★

Lesley

Source: Ken Hom

Ingredients

1 ½ lb (675g) fresh spinach

1 tbsp (15ml) peanut or vegetable oil

4 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed salt

1 tsp (sml) sugar

Directions

1. Wash the spinach thoroughly. Remove all the stems, leaving just the leaves. Heat a wok or a large skillet over high heat until it is hot.
2. Add the oil, and when it is very hot and slightly smoking, add the garlic and salt and stir-fry for 25 seconds.
3. Add the spinach leaves and stir-fry for about 2 minutes to coat them thoroughly with .the oil, garlic and 1 tsp (sml) of salt.
4. When the spinach has wilted to about one-third of its original volume, add the sugar and continue to stir-fry for another 4 minutes.
5. Transfer the spinach to a ·plate and pour off any excess liquid. Serve hot or cold.
