Protein Vanilla Cupcakes (160 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★

Lesley

Source: Pernilla

Ingredients

1 cup (80g) oat flour (I blend regular oatmeal)

1/3 cup (113g) 0% fat greek yogurt

1/2 cup-ish (92g) egg whites

2 scoops vanilla protein powder

3 tbsp truvia stevia (or sweetener of own choice)

1 tsp vanilla extract

2 tbsp water

1 tsp baking soda


Frosting & topping:

1 cup 0% fat greek yogurt

1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

1 tbsp truvia stevia

1/2 cut up chocolate Built Bar (or whatever topping you prefer)

Directions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F or/175 °C
2. Add all ingredients for the cupcake into a bowl and stir well.
3. Place 12 muffin liners into a muffin pan and divide batter into the 12 liners
4. Bake for about 12-16 minutes or until your vanilla cupcakes are fully baked.
5. Mix the frosting (without the sprinkles) and place in the fridge till the cupcakes are out of the oven
and have cooled down.
6. Frost and sprinkle your cupcakes and ENJOY!
