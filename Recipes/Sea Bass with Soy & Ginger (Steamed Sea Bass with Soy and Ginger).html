Ingredients

1 x 900 g whole wild seabass

20 g shredded ginger

20 g shredded spring onion

5 g fresh coriander leaf

5 g fresh red chillies

HOMEMADE SOY DRESSING

120 ml Lee Kum Kee soy sauce

8 ml Maggie seasoning sauce

50 g rock sugar

50 ml water

20 ml Shaoxing Chinese wine

10 g Knorr chicken powder

Description

Wtf?

Directions

Arrange the shredded ginger on the fish and steam it for 10 minutes.

Remove the fish from the steamer.

Re-heat the home-made soy sauce, if you made it earlier and pour it onto the fish.

Garnish with spring onion, red chillies and coriander leaf.

HOMEMADE SOY DRESSING

Mix some coriander stems, ginger and spring onion plus the soy ingredients and simmer slowly for 25 minutes.
