Turkey Apple Cheddar Mayo Toast (123 Kcal For 1)

★★★★★

Lesley

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Servings: 1 Source: eatingwell.com

Ingredients

2 slices fancy bread

1 tablespoon kewpie mayonnaise

2 ounces low- or reduced-sodium deli turkey or chicken

1 ounce sliced Cheddar cheese

4 to 6 thin apple slices

Description

SO GOOD - honestly, can replace apple as the crust.

Directions

Spread mayonnaise on both slices of bread. Top with turkey, Cheddar and apple.
