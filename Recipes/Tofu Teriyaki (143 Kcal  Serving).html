Tofu Teriyaki (143 Kcal / Serving)

★★★★

Lesley

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

300 g silken tofu

2 tbsp cornstarch

2 tsp oil


Teriyaki sauce:

¼ cup (60 ml) sake

¼ cup (40 ml) water

2 tablespoons soy sauce - regular

3 tablespoons mirin*

1 tablespoon sweetener

2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger (¾-inch/2 cm piece)

1 teaspoon sesame oil

2 garlic cloves, grated or minced

Directions

Cut 300 g (10½ oz) tofu (I prefer silken tofu) into 4 equal pieces and place on a double layer of paper towel. Drain for 10 minutes, then replace the paper towel and flip the tofu.

Spread 2 tablespoons cornflour (cornstarch) on a tray and dust the tofu all over.

Heat 2 teaspoons oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add the tofu and cook on both sides for 2–3 minutes.

Add the sauce to the pan, turn down the heat to low, and simmer for 2 minutes.

Spoon 3 tablespoons teriyaki sauce (store-bought or homemade) on top of the tofu, turn and cook for 1 minute.

Notes

Do not use sweet soy sauce
