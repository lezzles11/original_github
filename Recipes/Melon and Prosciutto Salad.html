Melon and Prosciutto Salad

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 to 12 Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

2 Cavaillon-style melons (Crenshaw are nice)

2 cups (100 g) picked purslane or watercress

Sea salt

4 tablespoons (60 ml) olive oil

12 slices prosciutto

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Directions

Cut the melons in half and scoop out the seeds. Using a vegetable peeler, remove the outer husk. With a sharp knife, cut the melon into long, thin slices.

On a large (12-inch/30.5 cm) platter, arrange the purslane and the slices of melon. Sprinkle with salt and the oil.

Deftly weave the prosciutto among the melon and purslane.

Sprinkle with lemon juice and serve at room temperature.
