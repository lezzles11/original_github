Smoked Camembert, Nuts, and Dried Figs with Rosemary

Source: Donabe

Ingredients

1 (8-ounce/240 g) good-quality Camembert cheese

Handful of smoke chips (your choice)

4 to 6 dried figs, halved

10 to 12 unsalted walnut halves

10 to 12 dry-roasted unsalted pistachios in their shells

2 to 3 sprigs rosemary

Directions

Remove the Camembert from the refrigerator 30 minutes before using. Place the Camembert on a piece of aluminum foil and wrap it so that only the top half is exposed.

Set the smoke chips in the donabe smoker according to the basic smoking instructions. Place the rosemary lightly over the smoke chips in the center.

Set up the middle and top grates with the ingredi-ents, placing the dried figs on the middle grate and the Camembert, walnuts, and pistachios on the top grate. Set the donabe, uncovered, over high heat.

Wait until the smoke chips start to release smoke, about 7 to 8 minutes. Cover, and fill the reservoir in the rim of the base a little over half full with water.

Smoke for 5 minutes over high heat, and then turn off the heat. Let rest undisturbed for 10 minutes.

Transfer the cheese, nuts, and figs to a serving plate and serve immediately.
