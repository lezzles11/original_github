Mashed Potatoes

★★

Dad

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 45 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 Source: Le Cordon Bleu

Ingredients

4 medium floury potatoes

250 ml (8 fl oz) milk or cream

20 g (¾ oz) unsalted butter or margarine

Directions

Peel the potatoes, cut into quarters and place in a large pan of salted water. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30–35 minutes, or until tender to the point of a knife.

Heat the milk or cream to boiling point, then remove from the heat and set aside.

Drain the potatoes and transfer to a large heat-resistant bowl. Mash with a large fork or potato masher, gradually mixing in enough butter and hot milk to give a good consistency, ensuring there are no lumps. Season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
