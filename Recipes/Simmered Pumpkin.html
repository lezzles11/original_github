Simmered Pumpkin

★★★

Lesley

Ingredients

- 600g of Japanese pumpkin, seeded, washed, and left unpeeled

- Water (200ml)

- Sugar (2 Tbsp)

- Mirin (1 Tbsp)

- Light soy sauce (1 tsp)

- Salt (a pinch)

- Toasted white sesame seeds

Directions

1. Cut pumpkin into 5 x 6cm pieces
2. Put pumpkin, water and sugar in a medium saucepan, then cover with a lid. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
3. Add mirin, light soy sauce and salt. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-10 minutes until pumpkin has softened. Remove from heat.
4. Dish out, then sprinkle sesame seeds on top and serve
