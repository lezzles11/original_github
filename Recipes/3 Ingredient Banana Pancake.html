Ingredients

12 tbsp (184g) Egg whites

1 small scoop (30g) Whey protein powder

1 medium (100g) banana

1/2 tbsp baking powder

Directions

1. Mash up the banana in a bowl.

2. Add the remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.

3. Heat a non-stick pan over low heat. (Use cooking spray as needed)

4. Scoop some of the mixture onto the pan and let the pancakes cook for 2-3 minutes on each side. 5. Serve with any toppings you like.(See Note)
