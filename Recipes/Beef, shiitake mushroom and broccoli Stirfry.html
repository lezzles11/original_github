Beef, shiitake mushroom and broccoli Stirfry

Source: Signe Johansen

Ingredients

1 tbsp oil

1 tsp soy sauce

1 tsp rice vinegar

1 tsp ginger

2 garlic cloves

1 tsp honey

120g beef

Shiitake Mushrooms

4 broccoli stalks

1 fresh red chilli

1 spring onion

Toasted sesame oil

Egg or rice noodles

Directions

1. Mix oil, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, garlic and honey / brown sugar in a bowl, then add beef strips
2. Marinate for few minutes
3. Place wok over high heat, add marinated beef
4. Remove beef
5. Add mushrooms & broccoli, stir fry
6. Add chilli and spring onion
7. Season with sesame oil, soy sauce / vinegar. Add sesame seeds on egg / rice noodles
