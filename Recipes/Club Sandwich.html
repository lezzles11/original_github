Club Sandwich

Difficulty: Easy Source: app.ckbk.com

Ingredients

Slices of bread — preferably two day old sliced by hand (see toast)

Butter for spreading

Mayonnaise mixed with a touch of English mustard

Thinly sliced freshly cooked chicken breast, lightly seasoned (the best way is on a plancha)

Fried egg, some people use boiled eggs but I prefer the taste and texture of a nice fried egg.

Crispy grilled bacon

Thinly sliced tomatoes

Iceberg lettuce

A whole green olive and a small pickle with food picks to hold the triangles in place

Directions

Take three slices of toast and butter them.

On the bottom slice spread with a little mayonnaise and then lettuce followed by the chicken and then the bacon. This is the chicken and lettuce under the bacon. Top with the tomato and the boiled egg — or fried egg if you follow my suggestion.

Add the second slice of toast buttered side up and repeat stage 2 minus the extra egg.

Top with the final piece of toast buttered side down.

Cut the sandwich into 4 triangles by cutting in diagonals and secure each triangle with a food pick and skewered with an olive. Serve as quickly as possible.
